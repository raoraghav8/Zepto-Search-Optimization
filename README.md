
# Zepto Search-to-Cart Conversion Optimization

## Overview

This project is a Product Management case study focused on analyzing and improving Zepto's Search-to-Add-to-Cart conversion funnel.

Zepto, one of India's leading quick-commerce platforms, experienced a decline in Search-to-Add-to-Cart conversion, impacting revenue and customer experience. This case study identifies potential root causes, proposes product solutions, prioritizes initiatives, and defines success metrics to improve conversion and user satisfaction.

---

## Problem Statement

Search-to-Add-to-Cart conversion dropped by approximately 10% over a four-week period, resulting in significant revenue impact.

### Key Questions

* Why are users dropping off after searching for products?
* Which user pain points contribute most to conversion loss?
* What product improvements can increase Add-to-Cart conversion?

---

## Objectives

* Identify friction points in the search journey.
* Analyze possible business and user-related causes.
* Recommend high-impact product improvements.
* Prioritize solutions using a structured framework.
* Define measurable success metrics.

---

## Approach

### 1. Root Cause Analysis

Potential reasons for conversion decline included:

* Out-of-stock products appearing in search results.
* Poor visibility of Add-to-Cart CTA buttons.
* Irrelevant search rankings.
* Ineffective filtering and sorting experience.
* Seasonal demand fluctuations.
* Casual browsing behavior.

### 2. User Research & Personas

Created representative user personas to understand:

* Shopping intent
* Purchase urgency
* Search behavior
* Decision-making patterns

### 3. User Journey Mapping

Mapped the complete journey:

Search → Product Discovery → Product Selection → Add to Cart

Identified drop-off points and friction areas throughout the funnel.

### 4. Solution Generation

Proposed multiple product improvements including:

* Smart out-of-stock substitutions
* Improved search ranking algorithms
* Better CTA visibility
* Personalized search results
* Enhanced filtering experience
* Contextual recommendations

### 5. Prioritization

Used the RICE Framework to prioritize solutions based on:

* Reach
* Impact
* Confidence
* Effort

Secondary Metrics:

* Bounce Rate

* Session Duration
